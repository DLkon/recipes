Coxinha is a Brazilian savory snack, of São Paulo origin, also common in Portugal, made with wheat flour pasta and chicken broth, which involves an elaborate filling with.

Ingredients

  • 5 breads
  • 2 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 tablespoon of cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoon of cheese grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoonn of roasted garlic
  • 1 spoon of parsley

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients until you reach the point of a homogeneous paste;
  2. Make flat cuts in the bread to fill it;
  3. Add the prepared paste to the breads and bake at 180° for 25 minutes;
  4. Taste!
